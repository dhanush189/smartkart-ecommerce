About SmartKart

Your complete online shopping experience in one simple app.

SmartKart is built for customers who want to discover products, compare details, add favorites to the cart, choose a delivery address, pay securely, and keep track of every order from one clean place.

Start Shopping Create Account Contact

Why SmartKart

From browsing electronics to downloading invoices after payment, SmartKart connects the full buying journey with admin tools that keep products, orders, and delivery updates organized behind the scenes.

Browse With Confidence

Explore latest products, trending categories, detailed descriptions, images, prices, and search filters so you can find the right item faster.

Cart And Checkout

Add products to your cart, update quantities, select only the items you want to buy, choose a saved delivery address, and complete payment through Razorpay.

Orders And Invoices

After checkout, you can view order details, check payment and delivery status, continue shopping, and download invoices whenever you need them.

Full Application

Built For Customers, Admins, And Owners

U

Customer Portal

Create an account, log in securely, browse the catalog, view product details, manage your cart, save addresses, place orders, and track purchases.

A

Admin Portal

Admins keep the store active by adding products, updating images, changing prices, managing categories, viewing customer orders, and updating delivery progress.

S

Super Admin Portal

The owner dashboard brings higher-level control with admin management, product oversight, order monitoring, and revenue tracking for the whole platform.

SK

One Connected Store

Every part works together: admins publish products, customers buy them, payments are recorded, invoices are generated, and orders stay visible until delivery.
